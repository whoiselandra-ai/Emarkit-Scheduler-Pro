# Privacy Policy for Emarkit

**Effective Date:** 1 November 2025

The owner of this application (“we”, “our”, or “us”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, store, use, and protect your information when you use Emarkit in conjunction with the TikTok API.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect and store the following types of data:

- **TikTok access tokens**: OAuth tokens required to interact with the TikTok API.  
- **User profile information**: TikTok username, account ID, avatar, and related metadata.  
- **Posted video information**: Video IDs, captions, thumbnails, and scheduling data.  
- **Uploaded video files**: Video files temporarily stored prior to posting.  
- **Analytics**: Metrics and insights from your content usage.  
- **Logs / Errors**: Technical logs and error reports to maintain service quality.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

- Upload and schedule videos to TikTok on your behalf.  
- Provide analytics and insights about posted content.  
- Improve the functionality, security, and reliability of Emarkit.  
- Troubleshoot and maintain system operations.

---

## 3. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 6).

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not sell** your personal data. Data may be shared with TikTok via the API as necessary for uploading and publishing content.

---

## 5. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

---

## 6. Your Rights

### GDPR / UK Users

- Access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.  
- Object to processing or request restriction.  
- Withdraw consent at any time.

### CCPA / California Users

- Request access to or deletion of personal information collected.  
- Opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell data).  
- Rights to equal service and price.

Requests can be sent to: **support@whoiselandra.com**

---

## 7. Third-Party Services

We use the TikTok API to provide functionality. TikTok’s privacy policy also applies to your data when interacting with TikTok.

---

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Updated versions will be posted at the same URL with the new effective date.
